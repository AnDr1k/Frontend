Chip tuning is not only an increase in power, for example, using chip tuning (changing the standard program in the engine control unit), you can disable faulty components of the engine management system, such as: DPF DPF FAP ADBLUE FLAPS particulate filters, speed limits, EGR valves, disable the catalyst control if it fails, you can also disable the rear lambda probes.
The use of chip tuning to increase power carries risks: loss of warranty, reduction in the resource of the engine, gearbox and other units, disruption of the operation of the ecological subsystems of the engine. After changes, more frequent maintenance and replacement of technical fluids are required.
